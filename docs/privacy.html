Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Your health data stays on your device and in your personal account. We do not sell your data to third parties, ever.

1. Information We Collect

PaceLife collects the following types of information to provide its service:

  • Account information: Your email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Check-in data: Energy scores, sleep hours, and mood ratings you log manually.
  • Health data: Step counts and sleep data read from Apple Health, with your explicit permission.
  • Location data: Your approximate location (with permission) to suggest nearby routes and fetch local weather.
  • Usage data: App interactions used to improve the product (e.g. which features you use).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to operate and improve PaceLife:

  • To generate your daily energy score and AI-powered insights.
  • To suggest walking and running routes based on your energy and local conditions.
  • To send you reminders and streak notifications (with your permission).
  • To display your progress, streak, and achievements over time.
  • To process your subscription and manage access to Pro features.

3. Apple Health

PaceLife requests access to Apple Health to read your step count and sleep data. This data is used only to enhance your energy score and insights. We never write data back to Apple Health without your explicit action. Health data is never shared with third parties.

4. Location

Location access is used to centre the map on your position, suggest nearby routes, and fetch local weather for your AI insight. Location data is not stored on our servers permanently. You can revoke location permission at any time from iOS Settings.

5. Data Storage

Your account and check-in data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the European Union. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Third-Party Services

PaceLife uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Apple WeatherKit — weather data
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI-generated insights (your check-in data is sent to generate insights; it is not used to train Anthropic's models)
  • Apple StoreKit — subscription management

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. You may also delete your account directly within the app, which will remove all associated data.

8. Children

PaceLife is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of PaceLife after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

privacy@inedgroup.com
