Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log a check-in?
Tap the + button on the Home screen or the "Log today's check-in" banner. You'll be asked to rate your energy (1–100), hours of sleep, and overall mood. Check-ins take less than 30 seconds.
Why isn't my step count showing?
PaceLife reads steps from Apple Health. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → PaceLife and make sure Steps is enabled. If you've recently installed the app you may need to reopen it after granting permission.
How does the AI insight work?
After you log a check-in, PaceLife sends your energy score, sleep, steps, and local weather to our AI model (Claude by Anthropic). It generates a personalised coaching insight for your day. Results are cached so you won't be charged extra API calls on refresh.
How do I restore a previous purchase?
Open the app and go to Profile → Subscription, then tap Restore purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase. If the issue persists, contact us with your order number.
How do I cancel my subscription?
PaceLife subscriptions are managed by Apple. To cancel, open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → PaceLife → Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of the billing period.
What is the free trial?
New subscribers get a 7-day free trial on both the Monthly and Annual plans. You won't be charged until the trial ends, and you can cancel at any time before then at no cost.
How do I delete my account and data?
To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at support@inedgroup.com with the subject line "Delete my account" from the email address linked to your account. We'll process the request within 7 days.
Why is the map not showing my location?
PaceLife needs location permission to centre the map and suggest nearby routes. Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → PaceLife and select "While Using the App" or "Always".
